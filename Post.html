About

Post Malone, AKA, Austin Richard, is an American rapper, singer, songwriter, and record producer. Born in Syracuse, New York and raised in Grapevine, Texas, Post made a name for himself, and has become one of the biggest hip hop phenomenons ever.

Music

Post Malone's music has been described as the "melting pot" of the country, grunge, hip hop, rap, and R&B. He even described himself as versatile and all over the place.

Background

Post has claimed that he was partitally Native American. He was also raised by his fath, Rich Post, and his stepmother, Hodie Post. When Post Malone was around 9 years old, his family moved to Grapevine, Texax. His father became the manager of concessions for the Dallas Cowboys, so they had to move down there. During his stay here, he was influenced by a lot of music and then inspired, slowly started to build a name for himself

Twitch

Post Malone got interested in gaming in his spare time from doing music. He then started streaming his gaming experiences live on a platform called Twitch. His audience always tuned in to watch him play all kinds of games, and were delighted to see the other half of his life.
